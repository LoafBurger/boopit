## Gameplay Overview

You control a glowing sci-fi orb with a directional energy blaster.  
Enemies spawn from all edges of the arena and home toward you.

### Your abilities:
- **Move:** WASD  
- **Aim:** Mouse  
- **Shoot:** Left Click  
- **Dash:** Left Shift  
  - Long neon streak dash  
  - Cooldown indicator on HUD  
- **Speed Boost Power-Up:** Temporary, increased movement speed  
- **Pause Menu:** ESC ‚Üí Resume / Main Menu / Quit
